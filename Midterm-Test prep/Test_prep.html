Truth Sweet Truth

Hello good people, how are you today? I was just thinking in the last few moments about how the truth sets people free. It was as though I was the Greek philosopher Pithagoreus jumping out of the bath exclaiming "Eureka!". Nothing, I mean nothing, is as sweet as the truth.

What is it that constitutes this life? When one confronts this, one returns to childhood. That is the first step to rejuvenation. For if one keeps life clear, and maintains pride in honour, one will be able to make life sweet. What else can focus a person as truth does? What else can make a person truly happy? I do not know! Does anyone?....

Can friendships be made without it? Can they be kept? Can one raise their voice other than as a desparate last cry without it? What can one say to the aching core muscle, the dear heart? How will ones mind act? How would Human tribes form?

So my friends, light a candle! Light a candle and hold it in solidarity for truth. Nothing shall stand in your way but this old theme of life.
